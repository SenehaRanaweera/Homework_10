Payment method of the customers

In the Australian telecommunication industry,several convenient payment methods are commonly used. These include bank transfers, allowing customers to directly transfer funds from their accounts, and credit card payments, which offer flexibility and quick processing. Electronic checks provide a secure, paperless alternative to traditional checks, while medical checks may be used for specific health-related services requiring verification or invoicing through medical entities. These payment options cater to diverse customer preferences, ensuring ease and accessibility.

Payment method according to gender

When analyzing the bar charts, it is clear that both male and female customers predominantly use electronic checks as their preferred payment method. This indicates a growing trend towards digital, paperless transactions in the telecom industry. In addition to electronic checks, other widely-used electronic payment methods include mobile banking apps, online payment portals, and digital wallets like PayPal. These options provide customers with secure, fast, and convenient ways to manage their telecom payments, highlighting the increasing shift towards electronic solutions in the Australian telecommunications sector.
